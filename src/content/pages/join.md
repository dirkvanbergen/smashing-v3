## How it works

1. **Get in touch** using the form — tell us a bit about you.
2. **Come to a free trial** training session with the right team.
3. **Sign up** as a member if it's a good fit. That's it!

## Membership

We're a non-profit club, so fees just cover hall hire, coaching and league costs. Youth memberships are discounted, and no one is turned away for money reasons — just ask.

- Adults — from €15 / month
- Youth (U16 & U14) — from €8 / month
- First trial session always free
