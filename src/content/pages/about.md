Smashing '72 is a small non-profit volleyball club. We welcome players of every age and ability — from first-timers picking up a ball for the first time to seasoned competitors chasing promotion.

## Our mission

To make volleyball accessible, affordable and fun for our whole community, while fielding competitive teams that make the club proud.

## What we offer

- Recreational and competitive teams for adults
- Youth development groups (U14 & U16)
- Weekly training sessions with qualified coaches
- A friendly, social club atmosphere off the court

## Get involved

Whether you want to play, coach, or volunteer, there's a place for you. [Reach out](/contact) and we'll help you find it.
